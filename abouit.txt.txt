Matematik asos:

* Bir kamerali model:
  dC/dt = -k * C

* Ikki kamerali model:
  dC_depo/dt = -ka * C_depo
  dC_qon/dt = ka * C_depo - ke * C_qon

* Eyler usuli:
  y_n+1 = y_n + h * f(t_n, y_n)

* Runge-Kutta 4-tartibli usuli:
  k1 = f(t_n, y_n)
  k2 = f(t_n + h/2, y_n + h*k1/2)
  k3 = f(t_n + h/2, y_n + h*k2/2)
  k4 = f(t_n + h, y_n + h*k3)
  y_n+1 = y_n + h/6 * (k1 + 2*k2 + 2*k3 + k4)
